% ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
% Front matter.  Title, then the four things a reader is owed before Chapter 1: the
% argument (preface), the book's one methodological commitment about itself (the numbers),
% the symbols (notation), and how to run it (reproduction — the short version; Appendix C
% is the long one).  \input rather than \include: these are not chapters and must not
% start their own aux files.
% ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

\part{Foundations}

\include{chapters/foundations}

% ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
% Part II · Methods.  One chapter per notebook, numbered as BOOK_SPEC.md plans them: a
% chapter keeps its number whether or not its neighbours have landed, so cross-references,
% equation numbers and float numbers are stable across a half-written book.
%
% Chapters 3 (uplift) and 8 (media mix) are guarded by \IfFileExists: they are authored
% concurrently, and a book that will not compile until every chapter is finished cannot be
% read until every chapter is finished. The guard drops out of the source the moment both
% files exist, and the counters around it are already correct for them.
% ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
\setcounter{part}{1}
